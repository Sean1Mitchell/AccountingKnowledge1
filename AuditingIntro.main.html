Welcome to Auditing

Auditing, from an accountant’s perspective, is the independent examination and evaluation of an organization’s financial statements, records, and internal controls to ensure accuracy, compliance, and integrity. The primary objective of auditing is to provide assurance to stakeholders—such as investors, regulators, and management—that the financial statements present a true and fair view in accordance with relevant accounting standards and laws.

In our topics, we're going to be exploring many of the tools in which Auditors use to do exactly that! this includes planning audits, gather evidence, reviewing internal controls etc.
