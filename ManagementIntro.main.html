Welcome to Management Accounting

Management accounting is a branch of accounting focused on providing financial and non-financial information to managers within an organisation to help them make informed business decisions. This helps businesses improve efficiency through better cost control, support strategic decisions like lanuching a new product, or entering a new market, to anticipate problems and manage them, as well as track performaces of staff, goals etc.

In our topics, we're going to be exploring many of the tools in which Management Accountants use to do exactly that! this includes budgets, variances, managing working capital etc.
