Welcome to Investments

Investments, from an accountant’s perspective, refer to the allocation of funds into assets or financial instruments with the expectation of generating income, capital appreciation, or both over time. For businesses, investments can include short-term instruments like marketable securities, or long-term assets such as property, plant, and equipment, as well as equity holdings in other companies.

In our topics, we're going to be exploring many of the aspects in which those in Investment roles use to do exactly that! this includes bond markets, understanding companies capital structures, diverse investment portfolios etc.
