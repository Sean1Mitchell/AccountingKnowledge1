Welcome to Taxation

Taxation, from an accountant’s perspective, refers to the process of managing, calculating, and reporting taxes in compliance with the applicable laws and regulations set by tax authorities. Accountants play a crucial role in ensuring that businesses and individuals meet their tax obligations accurately and on time while minimizing liabilities through legitimate tax planning strategies.

In our topics, we're going to be exploring many of the aspects in which Taxation Accountants need to know to do exactly that! this includes working our corporation tax and managing losses, and things like personal income tax, NIC, pensions and BiKs etc.
